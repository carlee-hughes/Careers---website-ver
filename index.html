Introduction

Computer science is a field of study that has grown in popularity in recent years, due to programming being afforded more recognition for its impact on the world, technological advancements speeding up to the point where it's difficult to keep track, and a growing demand for people with the technological skills to navigate the new world being forged. Needless to say, you've likely clicked on this website because you're considering pursuing computer science as a field of study.

However, 'computer science' is actually a grossly broad term, and there are many jobs you can get with a degree relating to it, and many degrees that do. This website will provide you with pertinent information regarding computer science career opportunities, learning opportunities, requirements and more.

Why Get Involved in Computer Science?

  • Computer science will shape the future
  • There is a growing demand for people with these skills
  • Advanced technology has woven itself into every aspect of our modern existence, and all of them use coding.
  • Computer scientists are needed in almost every industry
  • Develops your problem-solving, critical thinking and complex analytical skills
    • Well respected career choice
  • Stable salary and work-life balance

These are just SOME of the many reasons choosing computer science as a field of study is worth it. To learn more, explore this website.
