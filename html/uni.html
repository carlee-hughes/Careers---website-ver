University of Ottawa

75 Laurier Ave E, Ottawa, On K1N 6N5

The University of Ottawa offers several programs related to computer science. Here are two examples:

Software Engineering

Courses Required

  • English 4U or Françai 4U
  • Advanced Functions 4U
  • Calculus and Vectors 4U
  • Any of the following:
    • Biology 4U
    • Chemistry 4U
    • Computer Science 4U
    • Physics 4U

Grades Required

  • A minimum average of 70% is required for the prerequisite courses
  • Your minimum overall average should be roughly an 87%

Program Length

Four years
